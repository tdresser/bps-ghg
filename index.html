2020-21 Energy Intensity

INFO

All data presented here is from the Energy use and greenhouse gas emissions for the Broader Public Sector dataset as provided by the Ontario Ministry of Energy. Accurracy of the data is dependant on the source data, and no guarentee is made with respect to these results. Heating degree days are calculated separately for each board, based on the Weather Canada weather station located most appropriately for the board's geography. This website is intended to make this publically-available dataset easy to interact with and interpret. There is no affiliation with the Ministry of Energy.
